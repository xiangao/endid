# endid: Engression-Based Distributional Difference-in-Differences

Combines Lee & Wooldridge (2025) panel DiD transformations with
engression distributional regression to produce quantile treatment
effects (QTE), conditional average treatment effects (CATE), and
counterfactual distributions. Implements unit-specific residualization
and uses engression for nonlinear distributional estimation. Supports
both common-timing and staggered adoption designs with bootstrap
inference.
